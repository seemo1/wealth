Contest Features
A trading contest in which college students worldwide compete in!
  • Ranked by trading strategies instead of luck
  • You can earn: global ranking, cash prizes, professional training, and job opportunities
  • Winners have the chance to create funds, earn seeding and profit sharing from FDT!
Contest Rules
Round 1:
  • Contest date: 2016.2 ~ 2016.4
  • Contest duration: 8 weeks
Contest method:
  1. Player vs. player by school grouping in same region
  2. Make minimum 40 trades, 5+ trades in minimum 5 of 8 weeks
  3. The top 9 players (by FDT Score) from each school will advance to Round 2.
Round 2:
  • Contest date: 2016.5 ~ 2016.6
  • Contest duration: 8 weeks
Contest method:
  1. School team vs. school team in same region
  2. Teams must make minimum 80 trades, 5+ trades in minimum 5 of 8 weeks
  3. The team with the best FDT Score from each country advances to the championship in China.
Round 3:
  • Contest date: 2016.7 ~ 2016.8
Contest method:
  1. Teams compete with $100K live fund. There are 10 teams from China, US, UK, India, Taiwan, and Hong Kong.
  2. The school team with the highest score will become the winning team.
  3. Winning team can get 20% of their profits as prize. All of the teams in round 3 can continue to manage their live fund.
